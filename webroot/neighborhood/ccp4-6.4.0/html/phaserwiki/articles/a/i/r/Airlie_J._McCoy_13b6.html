Other Real McCoys

There are four other AJ McCoys publishing in biomedical science and cited in PubMed

  1. Andrea J. McCoy − University of Texas, TX USA and F Edward Hébert School of Medicine, Bethesda, MD USA and latterly also Division of Bacterial Pathogenesis, Graduate School of Medicine, University of the Ryukyus, Okinawa, Japan
  2. Almedia J. McCoy − East Carolina University, Greenville, NC USA
  3. Austin J. McCoy − Vanderbilt University, Nashville, TN USA
  4. A. Jeanene McCoy − Disney's Animal Programs, Walt Disney World Resort, FL USA

Thus an author search for McCoy AJ in PubMed will lead to a combined publication list for all five of us. Only the publications listed above are this real McCoy's!
